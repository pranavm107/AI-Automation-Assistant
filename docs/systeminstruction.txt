--------------------------------------------------

↓
API Layer
↓
Business Logic Layer
↓
AI Services Layer
↓
Data Layer

Database access must be isolated.

AI interactions must be isolated inside AI services.

--------------------------------------------------
RAG DESIGN RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Document Flow:

Upload Document
↓
Extract Text
↓
Chunk Text
↓
Generate Embeddings
↓
Store in FAISS
↓
Retrieve Relevant Chunks
↓
Send Context to Gemini
↓
Generate Response

Never bypass retrieval.

Always retrieve context before answering document-related questions.

Maintain source traceability whenever possible.

--------------------------------------------------
